Agile Tour is a series of non-profit events over several cities throughout October to December.

Join us for the 2015 edition of the Gatineau-Ottawa Agile Tour, affectionately known as GOAT. Organized by a local team of dedicated Agile practitioners, #GOAT15 will be an value-packed conference for the 300+ professionals expected to attend this year.

Program

We are building the program now and looking for session proposals. There are four tracks, each with 4 sessions — for a total of 16 sessions.
